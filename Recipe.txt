cut 8 oz of choclate in small chunks {save half melt half}
in a bowl put 3/4 cup of butter
then add 1 1/4 cup of grinded sugar
after that add 2 eggs
after that put 2 tsp of vanilla extract
then mix everything with a spatula.
then add the 4 oz of melted choclate.{do it slowly and not for too long so it does not burn}
after that add 3/4 cup of flour
then add 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
at last add 1 tsp of salt
then mix again
atlast add the choclate chunks that you saved.
 then bake it in a mold with butter paper inside it for 350 °F or 180 °C for 20-25 min
